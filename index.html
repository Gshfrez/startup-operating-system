Market and product thinking

Diagnose demand before you overbuild.

Market analysis checklist

  • TAM/SAM/SOM and realistic reachable market
  • Market timing and why-now catalyst
  • Market pull versus technology push
  • Buyer, user, champion, budget owner
  • Willingness to pay and switching costs
  • Distribution channels and trust barriers
  • Competition, substitutes, and moats
  • Regulatory, privacy, and compliance constraints

Product thinking checklist

  • Identify real pain through past behavior
  • Distinguish painkiller from vitamin
  • Define ICP, buyer, user, and use cases
  • Design MVPs around riskiest assumptions
  • Prioritize features by learning and value
  • Use feedback without obeying every request
  • Track activation, retention, referral, revenue
  • Avoid fake progress and vanity metrics
